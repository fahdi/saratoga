AMBER OAKS
Park Place

Amber Oaks

3501 CENTURY OAKS ROAD

DURHAM, NC 27713

Amber Oaks Apartments (originally Country Scene Apartments) is a 96 unit garden
apartment community constructed in 1986 located in Durham, NC.

This property was acquired in late 2012. Immediately after closing, ownership

embarked on a $450,000 improvement program addressing deferred maintenance as
well as performing interior unit renovations.

Specific asset management strategies employed to enhance revenue and tenant
quality:

UNIT UPGRADES

Full Renovation Strategy: Fully renovate units as tenants vacate

Partial Renovation Strategy: Offer customized renovation packages to existing
tenants who have a good payment history. This program allows for a faster execution
of the renovation program while simultaneously reducing downtime resulting in a
lower vacancy rate.

EVICTIONS/DELINQUENCY

Enact an aggressive eviction program to remove not only non-paying tenants but
systematic late payers.

AUTOMATION OF RUBS SYSTEM

Exchanged manually read water meters to a wireless automated system. This system
resulted in an increase in monthly collections of water & sewer charges as well as

CURB APPEAL ENHANCEMENT

Resurfacing the parking field, installing a new playground, converting the tennis
court into sport and restoring and reopening the pool after being closed for 5 years.
