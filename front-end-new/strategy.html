STRATEGY

We capitalize on favorable economic and demographic trends for multifamily apartment complexes in the United States by targeting the middle market segment of select regions in the mid-Atlantic and Southeast United States.

We make select
acquisitions by
applying the
following criteria:

OWNERSHIP/MANAGEMENT

Properties with poorly capitalized ownership and/or poor existing management personnel and practices.

PROPERTY LOCATION, AGE AND VISIBILITY

Properties in submarkets with consistent strong occupancy

FAVORABLE MARKET DYNAMICS

Economic drivers (minimum of 2 local drivers must be present in a market in order to commit to an acquisition)

We enhance value and performance through operational improvements, systematic property upgrades, enhanced curb appeal and intensive asset and property management.
